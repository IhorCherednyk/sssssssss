Zwipe
payments solution

Biometrics in the banking and payment industries is already moving forward, with many organizations taking proactive steps to further protect and improve the way they interact with customers and end users. With this focus, our innovative fingerprint payment card, not only prevents fraud but it also eliminates the need for pins, meaning that in less than 1 second you can complete a contactless transaction.

Our payment solutions enables consumers to have a more secure, faster and easier experience while also knowing that their biometric information is safe, as it is only on the users card and not in an external database.
